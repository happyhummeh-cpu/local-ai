# QEVRYN Responsible AI Policy
**Effective Date**: July 2026  
**Branding**: Built by Veeomdecoders

At QEVRYN, we believe in building technology that empowers humanity while safeguarding individual freedom and privacy.

## 1. Safety Guidelines & Intended Use
QEVRYN is designed as an offline-first assistant for productivity, document search, software coding, and education. You agree not to use the application to:
* Generate malicious code, cybersecurity threats, or phishing assets.
* Create deepfakes or misinformation intended to mislead or harm.
* Violate intellectual property rights or private licensing terms of any third-party code.

## 2. Fact Verification
Because AI models can hallucinate:
* Users are strongly encouraged to verify all medical statements, legal procedures, structural engineering calculations, and compiled codebases generated by QEVRYN before deployment.
* QEVRYN provides a dual-model comparison or web grounding reference whenever available to assist with verification.

## 3. Transparency
QEVRYN will always make it clear when an answer is generated, when web research has been blended into an answer, and which local or remote core was routed to process the prompt.
