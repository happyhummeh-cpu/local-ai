**Version**: 0.1

QEVRYN is a model-agnostic AI orchestrator. It supports various open-source and commercial foundation models. This document lists the licensing and usage obligations of supported models.

* **Meta Llama 3 / Llama 3.1**: Subject to the Meta Llama 3 Community License. By downloading or utilizing Llama models, you agree to Meta's acceptable use policies.
* **Gemma 2 / Gemma**: Released under the Google Gemma Terms of Use.
* **Mistral / Mixtral**: Released under the Apache 2.0 License.
* **OpenAI Whisper (via whisper.cpp)**: Released under the MIT License.

## 2. Commercial / Cloud API Models
* **Google Gemini Series (Gemini 3.5 Flash, Gemini 3.1 Pro)**: Governed by the Google APIs Terms of Service and Gemini API Services Terms.
* Cloud model integrations are optional and deactivated by default.

## 3. Compliance and Redistribution
* QEVRYN does **not** pre-package or redistribute any proprietary model binaries or weights directly in the core installer.
* All models are downloaded dynamically on-demand from official community repositories (HuggingFace, Ollama Registry) with explicit user consent.
